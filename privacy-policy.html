Privacy Policy — Coffee Shop

Effective date: 09-13-2025

Introduction

Coffee Shop ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use and protect it, and your choices. By visiting our shop, using our website, signing up for communications, or otherwise interacting with us, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information collected automatically:

Personal information you provide

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number) when you sign up for newsletters or loyalty programs.
  • Payment information when you place orders or pay in-store (we do not store full card numbers; payments are processed via secure third-party processors).
  • Order details and preferences (items purchased, delivery address if applicable).
  • Feedback, reviews, or messages you send us.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and browser information (e.g., IP address, device type, browser version).
  • Usage data and analytics when you visit our website (pages visited, time on site, referrer).
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see "Cookies" below).

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill orders, payments, refunds, and exchanges.
  • To communicate with you about your orders, reservations, or inquiries.
  • To provide customer service and respond to feedback.
  • To send promotional messages and offers if you opt in to receive them.
  • To improve our menu, services, and website through analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and property.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: payment processors, point-of-sale providers, delivery partners, email and marketing platforms, and analytics providers that assist us in operating our business.
  • Legal & safety: if required by law, to respond to legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our customers or the public.
  • Business transfers: in the event of a merger, sale, reorganization, or acquisition of assets (we will notify affected users when required).

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect personal information. While we strive to protect your data, no system is completely secure. If a data breach occurs, we will take steps required by law and notify affected individuals as appropriate.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Specific retention periods depend on the type of information and purpose.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to provide and improve our services, analyze site usage, and deliver relevant marketing. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect site functionality.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate or unnecessary information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Object to or restrict processing in certain circumstances.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before acting on requests.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or a higher age where applicable). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to delete it.

Third-Party Links & Services

Our website or communications may contain links to third-party sites (e.g., online ordering platforms, social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Review their policies before providing personal information.

International Transfers

If you are located outside the country where our business operates, your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the country where our service providers or our company operate. We will take steps to ensure that such transfers comply with applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Effective Date" above and, where required by law, notify you.
